About the Conference

ICAM-2024 aims to promote innovation, facilitate knowledge exchange, and advance materials science across various applications. By bringing together leading scientists and researchers from around the world, this conference will provide a platform for exploring the latest advancements in the fields of semiconductors, energy storage, nanotechnology, environmental sustainability, and biomedicine.

About the Department

The Department of Materials Science at the University of Madras, established as an independent entity in 2014, was built on a rich legacy of research dating back to 1970. Our goal is to advance the field of materials science through a comprehensive approach that encompasses the synthesis, structure, performance, and application of materials. In 2018, we introduced a M.Sc. Materials Science program that emphasizes both theoretical and experimental aspects, with a strong focus on Physics. The curriculum delves into the intricate relationships between the composition, structure, processing, and properties of materials as well as atomic structures. We are committed to integrating cutting-edge science with national strategies and industrial needs to make us a leader in innovation.

About the University

Established in 1857, the University of Madras is one of the oldest and most prestigious institutions for higher learning in India. As the "mother" of several other universities in South India, it has a distinguished history of academic achievement and innovation.
